ABOUT ME

I am currently a second-year PhD candidate of the Artificial Intelligence Thrust Area, Information Hub at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), co-supervised by by Prof. James T. Kwok and Prof. Pan Hui. Before joining in HKUST, I researched the topic of reservoir computing (RC) and time series modeling with Prof. Qianli Ma and received my MA.Eng degree in the School of Computer Science and Engineering, South China University of Technology (SCUT). Before I came to SCUT, I received my B.S. degree from the department of mathematics at Jinan University (JNU) in July 2015.
